A Guide To Neptune

Location and Orbit

  • Neptune is the eighth planet from the sun and has the second lowest eccentric orbit, meaning it is almost perfectly circular.
  • It is located about 4.5 billion kilometers from the Sun.
  • Beyond Neptun's orbit lies the Kuiper Belt, a region of dwarf planets, comets, and other icy objects.

About

  • Neptune's famous blue color is from the methane in its atmosphere.
  • Having the highest wind speeds in the solar system, Neptune's storms are some of the strongest and most violent. With some vortices being as wide as Earth.
  • There are 16 known moons, with Triton being the largest and oddly enough having a retrograde orbit.
