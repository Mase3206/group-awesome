Stellar Tours

Welcome to our Stellar tours where you can learn about our Galaxy's stellar wonders! From constellations to the stars that make them up if you want a guide to the stars you're in the right place!

Learn about the Stars of our galaxy and what makes them tick or, whether it be the stars that compose them or the folklore that created them, explore the nature of Earth's constellations!

Betelgeuse

A Red Supergiant.
640 Light Years Away!

Polaris

A Supergiant Cepheid Star.
Also known as "The North Star".

Sirius

A White Dwarf Star.
The brightest star in the Earth's night sky!

The Big Dipper

Part of the larger constellation Ursa Major.
Known by many names, including "The Plough", "Great Wagon", and "Norther Dipper" to name a few.

Cygnus

Visible from both of Earth's hemispheres!
Associated with Greek Mythology.

Orion

Takes its name from Greek Mythology.
Contains two of the brightest stars in Earth's night sky!
