A Guide To Kepler 186f

Location and Orbit

  • Kepler 186f is a super Earth that orbits an M-type star.
  • It is about .43 AU from its host star and takes 129.9 Earth days to orbit once.
  • It has a mass of abou 1.17 Earth masses with an orbital eccentricity of .04.

About

  • Its star is a red dwarf and it is within the habitable zone of its star, Kepler 186.
  • Within the same system there are four smaller companion planets that orbit closer to the star.
  • Because of its proximity to its star there is a possibility for liquid water to exist on the surface.
